Aliucord Installer

⚠️ IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

- Supported Android Versions:

• Android 7 - Android 13

- Supported Architectures:

• arm, arm64, x86_64

Installation:

1.- Download the Aliucord Installer

2.- Open it

3.- Click "Install", then choose the "Download" Option.

4.- Wait for the installer to patch the Discord APK.

5.- Click "Install" once prompted by Android.

6.- If Play Protect warns you about the app being unsafe, ignore & dismiss it. This is because the app is built locally, so Play Protect doesnt recognize te signature.

7.- Open Aliucord, give access to files (this is for loading Aliucord & Plugins) log in, and now you have Aliucord.

Plugin Installation:

- Join our support server first:

https://discord.gg/EsNDvBaHVU,

then go to #plugins-list and check what do you want to install.

- Hold down the message (not the link), and click "Open Plugin Downloader"

- Find the plugin you want to install and click install. Some plugins may require a restart.
