Aliucord

A discord android modification.

Features:

• Robust plugin system.

- Installing, removing, using, enabling, disabling, and configuring plugins without rebuilding/reinstalling the app.

• No root needed.

• In app updater for Aliucord and Plugins.

• Aliucord automatically blocks almost all Discord tracking by default (this does not include FireBase since notifications rely on that)
